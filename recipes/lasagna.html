Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

It is rich, filling, and perfect for family meals.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
